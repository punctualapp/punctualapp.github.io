Questions, answered

Is it really free?

Yep. MIT-licensed and open source — no accounts, no subscriptions, no pro tier, no catch.

Which calendars?

Anything in macOS Calendar — Google, iCloud, Outlook, CalDAV. If your Mac sees it, Punctual sees it.

Which meeting links?

Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, Webex — plus plain URLs in the event notes.

Does my data leave my Mac?

Never. Everything runs locally — no servers, no telemetry. Read the source; that's the point.
